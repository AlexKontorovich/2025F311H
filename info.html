Course Structure

Until at least the first midterm, we will run an experimental "flipped" classroom experience:

  • Pre-Class: Students will watch recorded lectures in groups of their choosing
  • During Viewing: Pause, rewind, or speed up as needed; discuss questions with classmates
  • Notes Required: Write down key definitions, theorems, and proof ideas, plus any questions
  • Upload: Submit PDF scan of notes to Canvas before each meeting
  • Class Time: Review material, Q&A, discuss previous problem sets, and take brief quizzes
